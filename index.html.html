About Us

A Unique Experience, Every Time

Shiv Garden Resort is a premier destination in the heart of Darbhanga, Bihar. Nestled in lush greenery with a grand banquet hall and open lawn, we offer everything under one roof — from daily dining to dream weddings.

Our restaurant serves authentic Indian cuisine every day, welcoming families for lunch, dinner, and special occasions. Our grand hall and lawn have hosted hundreds of unforgettable weddings with capacity for 700+ guests.

From décor and catering to comfortable stay for wedding guests, our experienced team manages every detail so you can cherish every moment.
